About Us

mTek Services is an innovative Mobile Application - a platform which provides an entirely paperless ecosystem for the insurance industry. Through our digital platform, we offer insurance cover online to customers in

  • Motor
  • Medical
  • Evacuation
  • Travel
  • Golf
  • Personal Accident
  • Domestic

Our Insurance solution allows customers to purchase policies directly from the Insurer, file for claims directly from the convenience of their smart device thus increasing efficiency and can capture critical data on claims from geo-located accident scenes.
